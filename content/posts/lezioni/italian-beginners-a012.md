## Verbi (-ere) *Second conjugation*

### Prendere *to take*

*Stem + 'ere'. Keep the stem, change the ending*

- prend-o *I take*
- prend-i *You (singular) take*
- prend-a *He/she takes*
- prend-iamo *We take*
- prend-ete *You (plural) take*
- prēnd-ono *They take*
